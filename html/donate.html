Donate to our student scholarship program:

BOH is an organization that sustainably supports widows and orphans in Uganda, Africa. A part of our mission is to empower orphans and underprivileged children by providing them with the opportunity to obtain an education in Uganda. (Read more about our scholarship program here.) By making a donation today, your contribution will go directly towards our student scholarship program. Donors can contribute any single amount today, or you can sign up to support our students on a monthly basis. With our reoccurring donors, you may cancel your subscription at any time you choose. Donations made today will be tax-deductible as soon as we receive our 501(c)3, our non-profit status, from the government. Any contribution you provide is greatly appreciated by all our BOH members, here in the U.S. as well as in Uganda.
